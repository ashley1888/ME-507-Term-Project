void task_gripper_motor ( void *  p_params)

Gripper-motor task.

Turns on the gripper-motor once the z-motor tasks has completed. The gripper-motor will turn on for a specified length of time, wait a different length of time, then turn off the motor and indicate that the task has been completed.
